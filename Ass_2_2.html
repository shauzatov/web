Albert Einstein

About Einstein

Albert Einstein (1879-1955) was an outstanding theoretical physicist, author of the theory of relativity and winner of the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921. His discoveries have changed the fundamental understanding of space, time, and energy.

Major Life Events

  1. 1879 – Born in Ulm, Germany
  2. 1905 – He has published articles on the special theory of relativity
  3. 1921 – The Nobel Prize in Physics
  4. 1933 – Emigration to the USA
  5. 1955 – He died in Princeton, USA.

Key Contributions

  • Theory of relativity
  • The photoelectric effect (the basis of quantum mechanics)
  • Formula E = mc²
  • Research in the field of cosmology
